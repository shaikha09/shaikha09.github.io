A Day in the Life at NYUAD


This is how students like to spend their day at NYUAD. Whether it is the marketplace, Baraha, the library, the rooftop, or the palm trees. Each students have their favorite spot on campus.


In the heart of the campus, the marketplace is a hub of activity, where students gather to socialize, eat and drink, and engage in lively discussions. It's the perfect spot for forging new friendships and connecting with peers from around the world. For those who are seeking entertainment, the Baraha is the perfect place to lay back, relax and play ping pong with friends.


Underneath the palm trees, is the best place to relax and enjoy the scenery of AlSaaidyat Island especially during winter time. Most students spend their time there relaxing, chatting, and catching up with assignments.


NYUAD is not completed without the infamous library. Here, students often spend most their day from early morning to late night study seessions. The cozy architecture helps students to focus and engage with their work and academics.


Every student at NYUAD has their own favorite spot on campus, a place where they feel a unique connection to their university and the diverse community that thrives within it. These spaces not only shape their daily routines but contribute to their experiences that make NYUAD a home and a place to study and grow.
